Superman - Movie Review

Overview

Title: Superman
Director: James Gunn
Release Year: 2025th Edition
Rating: 7.3/10*

Plot Summary

When Superman gets drawn into conflicts at home and abroad, his actions are questioned, giving tech billionaire Lex Luthor the opportunity to get the Man of Steel out of the way for good. Will intrepid reporter Lois Lane and Superman's four-legged companion, Krypto, be able to help him before it's too late?

Review

James Gunn did an amazing job telling a story that was new, but still felt like a classic comic book.

Highlights

Visual Effects: Spectacular CGI that brings the adventure to life, especially during the black hole scene.
Acting: Amazing performances, especially the lead actor, David Corenswet, He made superman feel real.
Music: Perfect music to bring tensions to certain scenes.

Critic Notes

*the film's conflicting tones, with Gunn's signature self-aware humor and jokes undermining emotional moments and creating "tonal whiplash".
*Critics argue that the script tries to cram too many story elements, characters, and modern-day themes into the first solo film, making it overstuffed and unfocused.
